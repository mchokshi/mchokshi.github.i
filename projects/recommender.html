Melanoma Educational Intervention

Conducted translational research alongside Dr. Robert Smith, Dermatologist; Dr. Alexandra Leader, Emergency Medicine Physician; and two EVMS medical students to develop a Hispanic/Latinx community-based educational intervention to address health disparities in malignant melanoma. Curated educational products, presentations, and take-home patient literature in both the English and Spanish languages, drawing from validated sources such as the American Academy of Dermatology (AAD), Skin of Color Society (SOCS), and prior clinical studies.
